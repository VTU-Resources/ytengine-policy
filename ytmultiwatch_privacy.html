Privacy Policy

YT Multi Watch Engine does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

This app loads YouTube videos based on the links provided by users. It does not require login, and no user data is stored or transmitted from the app. We value your privacy and ensure no data is collected by us.

However, the app relies on YouTube (a service by Google LLC), and YouTube may collect data under its own policies when videos are played. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

We may show ads using Google AdMob to support the app, but we do not directly collect or track any personal data.

If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at: ytmultiwatchengine@gmail.com
